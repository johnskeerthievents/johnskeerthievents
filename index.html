About Us

John’s Event Planner is a premium event management company serving Pathanamthitta, Kottayam, Alappuzha, and Kollam, Kerala. We specialize in creating unforgettable weddings, birthdays, and special occasions with professional services including wedding planning, bridal makeup, catering, photography, videography, pre/post-wedding shoots, maternity shoots, baby photography, model portfolios, and Christian wedding choirs (Marthoma, Orthodox, Malankara Catholic). With exceptional attention to detail, creative designs, and reliable execution, we ensure your celebrations are seamless, stylish, and stress-free. From intimate gatherings to grand functions, John’s Event Planner brings your vision to life.

Be it a Wedding, Engagement, Birthday celebration, get together party, or any other event, there are some occasions in your life that you really want to enjoy to the fullest, that you have had dreams about, that you wish to gift you some of the most treasured memories in your life. With a wide range of services, creative and efficient planning, dedicated execution, we take away from you the tensions and worries and let you enjoy those occasions and give you an experience like none other.
